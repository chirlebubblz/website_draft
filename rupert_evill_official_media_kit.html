Official Biography

About Rupert Evill

"Keeping cross-border investors out of the headlines and in the black."

As a former counter-terrorism intelligence analyst and seasoned international investigator, Rupert has spent over 25 years in the trenches of risk across multiple sectors.

Trained by the elite behavioral specialists who advise global intelligence agencies, Rupert holds postgraduate qualifications in Behavioral Analysis and Deception Detection, achieving distinctions for evaluating truthfulness and credibility. He is a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE) and an accredited Crisis Responder who has spent thousands of hours in adversarial settings, resolving multi-million dollar whistleblower allegations, and responding to critical extortion demands.

Rupert uses this frontline experience—seeing exactly how human decision-making, distorted incentives, and cultural pressures cause chaos—to strip away the heavy, abstract busywork of traditional risk management and due diligence. He provides hyper-practical, counterintuitive insights to investors, boards, and executive teams navigating complex, high-risk environments.
